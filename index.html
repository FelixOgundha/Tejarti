About tejarti

Our Story

Tejarti was born in February 2020 as a social program to support e-commerce startup of housewives, young entrepreneurs, and people of determination (people with physical disabilities) in the UAE. Tejarti as an e-commerce enabler provided supportive solutions program visioning to help converting each startup business idea to an actual business with the least possible cost and effort.

What is Tejarti?

Tejarti is an Arabic word meaning “My Trade”. As the Trading business has been always part of the Arab Heritage for thousands of years. We chose the name to bridge the past glory of the Arabs Trade Journey with thriving Digital Economy in the MENA region.

We share the journey with you along the way to boost the sale of your business

We have assembled a team well-established in the e-commerce market from around the world. We’ve helped hundreds of clients get their stores up and running on Amazon and other marketplaces, so we're familiar with the challenges that small companies and individual clients face.

As we believe that, the insider knowledge and experience is a great tool to support our clients along the journey to success.

We believe anyone and everyone can have E-commerce business today!

Tejarti was founded by dedicated housewives and mothers that wanted to support their family without compromising their own family life. Hence in Tejarti, we believe that the current booming digital economy in the UAE in particular and the world in general, is closing the gab between the old entrepreneurship school and new self-made business success stories With limited investment you can as a staying home mother, a teen, a full time employee, a senior citizen and a person of determination start your e-commerce business today! Our team will help you to move forward with your business and seize the opportunity to join digital economy with less complication with much of clarity and honesty.
